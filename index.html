About Us

The coFounders of "8-mianka corp" are descendents of the ancient Bulgarian "Slanina" clan, united by the idea of creating a better world, they founded the "8-minaca corp" organization and "8-mianka" charity foundation in order to promote the "Slaninology", the ancient art of wellbeing and happiness.
